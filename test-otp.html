📋 Instructions

  1. Check Status: Click "Check OTP Service Status" to verify the service is running
  2. Generate OTP: Enter valid email and password, then click "Generate OTP"
  3. Check Email: Look for the OTP in your email inbox (check spam folder too)
  4. Verify OTP: Enter the same email and the 6-digit OTP code, then click "Verify OTP"

Note: If email is not configured, the OTP will be displayed in the server console logs.
